A pottery studio dedicated to creativity and community.

Sarah Glass Ceramics is a woman-owned pottery studio located in Fort Lauderdale. Our mission is to provide everyone of all skill levels a strong foundation and supportive environment to experience the relaxing art of pottery making. We are happy to provide studio space and equipment to let you channel your inner artist. Since opening in 2016, the studio has thrived with the support of the pottery community and continues to grow. Today, Sarah Glass Ceramics is nestled in a beautiful historic building on Progresso Drive, just a short walk from local coffee shops, restaurants, and bars, making it the perfect place to immerse yourself in creativity.
